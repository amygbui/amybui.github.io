Why software engineering?

I previously worked for CVS/Pharmacy as a certified pharmacy technician. I began seeing all the ways we could automate and streamline processes at every store I worked. Cutting down time on unnecessary tasks meant more time my team could devote to helping our patients. I wanted to be the person who shook up the industry.
